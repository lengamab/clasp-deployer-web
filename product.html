Frequently Asked Questions

What makes ScriptFlow different from using CLASP directly?

ScriptFlow provides a visual IDE layer on top of CLASP with live logs, version control UI, integrated debugging, and unified management of both Apps Script and automation platforms like Make.com and Zapier in one dashboard.

Can I use ScriptFlow with my existing Apps Script projects?

Absolutely. ScriptFlow connects to your existing Google Apps Script projects via OAuth. Import any project and start managing it through our unified dashboard immediately.

Does ScriptFlow support team collaboration?

Yes. ScriptFlow Pro and Enterprise plans include team workspaces, shared project access, role-based permissions, and collaborative debugging features.

How does the AI Blueprint Converter work?

Upload a Make.com or n8n workflow blueprint (JSON), and our AI engine analyzes the nodes, connections, and logic to generate equivalent Google Apps Script code. This allows you to run workflows on Google's free infrastructure, saving on execution costs.

Is there a free tier?

Yes. ScriptFlow offers a Free tier with basic project limits and core features. It's perfect for individual developers and small projects.

What integrations does ScriptFlow support?

ScriptFlow integrates with Google Apps Script, Make.com, Zapier, n8n, GitHub, Slack, and supports webhooks for custom integrations.
